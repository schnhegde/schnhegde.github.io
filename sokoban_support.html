Sokoban Masters

Sokoban is a simple and engaging game to train your brain.

Objective: Help to push boxes into designated storages with your intellect.

Features:

  • Has over 600+ levels, All for free

  • Touch/Swipe control

  • Progress to next level by solving each level

  • 3 stars for optimal moves

  • Timer to challenge yourself
